## 🌍 Community & Influences (Referenced Sites)
*I stand on the shoulders of giants. These are the open-source curriculums and fellow self-taught developers who inspire this site:*

  * **[OSSU (Open Source Society University)](https://github.com/ossu/computer-science):** The gold standard for a free, self-taught CS curriculum.
  * **[CS DIY Wiki (PKUFlyingPig)](https://csdiy.wiki/):** A brilliant comprehensive guide to self-learning CS.

  * **[Coding Interview University](https://github.com/jwasham/coding-interview-university):** A multi-month study plan for becoming a software engineer for a large company.

  * **[Nightmare](https://guyinatuxedo.github.io/):** An incredible intro course and resource for reverse engineering and binary exploitation.

  * **[Artoriuz OSEE](https://github.com/Artoriuz/OSEE):** An excellent and comprehensive 3 year curriculum to self-learn Electrical Engineering

  * **[Awesome Electronics](https://github.com/kitspace/awesome-electronics):** Amazing list of resources for learning Electronics.

  * **[Computer Science Lectures](https://github.com/Developer-Y/cs-video-courses):** A great online bookmarks/lookup table for freely available online video courses/lectures.

  * **[Reverse Engineering & Malware Analysis Roadmap](https://github.com/x86byte/RE-MA-Roadmap):** An amazing and comprehensive roadmap for mastering reverse engineering and malware analysis.

  * **[Awesome LLM Security](https://github.com/pedramamini/awesome-llm-security):** A curation of awesome tools, documents and projects about LLM Security.

  * **[InfoSec Reference](https://github.com/rmusser01/Infosec_Reference):** My favorite free resource for anyone interested in learning more about Information Security.

  * **[NYU Tandon's OSIRIS Lab's Hack Night](https://github.com/osirislab/Hack-Night):** Uumm! My 2nd favorite security resource.

  * **[Awesome Reversing](https://github.com/tylerha97/awesome-reversing):** A curated list of awesome reversing resources

  * **[Awesome Computer Science](https://github.com/quabanc/awesome-computer-science):** A curated lists of resources for various fields in Computer Science.

  * **[Cybersecurity Resources](https://github.com/alex-bellon/cybersecurity-resources):** A collection of links, notes, programs, websites, etc. related to CTFs and cybersecurity.
